Research Interests:

I am interested in the theory and practice of distributed systems. Together with colleagues: We are investigating scalable and dependable distributed systems, focussed on scaling the performance of the State Machine Replication approach. See publications below related to this research track.

Key-words: Distributed Systems, Replication, State Machine Replication (SMR), Parallel SMR, Partitioned SMR, Recovery, State-Transfer, Consensus Protocols, Multicast Protocols, Blockchains

In the past I have also investigated and proposed techniques and approaches to build correct distributed systems by construction. Here an (1 pg.) abridged description of our approaches and results.
